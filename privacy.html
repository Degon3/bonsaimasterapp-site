Privacy Policy

Bonsai Master is designed with privacy in mind.
Your data stays on your device.

Local-first by design

Bonsai Master stores your data locally on your device.

This includes:

  • Bonsai leaves
  • Stress score
  • Daily goal
  • Selected draining apps
  • Flexible days
  • Focus progress
  • App settings

No cloud storage is used.

App usage access

If you grant app usage access, Bonsai Master reads the total time spent in the apps you select.

The app does not read:

  • App content
  • Messages
  • Screens
  • Keystrokes

Notifications

Notifications are used only for reminders you explicitly enable, such as:

  • Focus reminders
  • Pruning reminders
  • Daily goal reminders

You can disable notifications at any time from your device settings.

No data selling or sharing

Your data is not sold, shared, or sent to third parties.

Bonsai Master:

  • Does not run ads
  • Does not use trackers

Reset and control

You can erase all local data at any time using:

Settings → Data & permissions → Start over
